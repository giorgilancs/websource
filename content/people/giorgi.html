Emanuele is a Senior Lecturer in Biostatistics and is currently the Head of CHICAS. He first joined CHICAS in October 2012, for a PhD in Statistics and Epidemiology under the supervision of Peter Diggle. After completing his PhD, Emanuele was awarded an MRC Fellowship in Biostatistics which was turned into a Lectureship in July 2018.

His research interests are in the development of novel spatial and spatio-temporal statisical methods for disease risk mapping, with a special focus on malaria and neglected tropical diseases.
